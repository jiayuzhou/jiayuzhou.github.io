Short Biography

Jiayu Zhou is a senior research scientist at Samsung Research America, working on Ads recommendation on Smart TV. Jiayu has a broad research interest in Machine Learning and Data Mining. Jiayu is the main author of the open-source multi-task learning software MALSAR (multi-task learning via structural regularization). The package has included solvers to many state-of-art regularization-based multi-task learning algorithms. The package is firstly introduced in my multi-task tutorial that was given at the Twelfth SIAM International Conferece on Data Mining (SDM'12).

Before joining Samsung Research, Jiayu was a research associate at Arizona State University under the supervision of Professor Jieping Ye. During his PhD study, Jiayu interned twice at IBM T.J. Watson Research Center where he designed novel machine learning models for healthcare analytics.
